## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI, or embodied AI, refers to artificial intelligence systems that interact with the physical world through sensors and actuators. Unlike traditional AI that operates purely in digital spaces, Physical AI systems must navigate the complexities of real-world physics, uncertainty, and dynamic environments.

Key characteristics of Physical AI include:

- **Embodiment**: Intelligence is shaped by the physical form and its interactions with the environment
- **Real-time Processing**: Systems must respond to environmental changes in real-time
- **Sensorimotor Integration**: Combining sensory input with motor output for intelligent behavior
- **Adaptation**: Ability to adapt to changing physical conditions and environments

## The Humanoid Robotics Approach

Humanoid robots offer unique advantages for exploring Physical AI:

- **Human-like Interaction**: Natural interfaces for human-robot collaboration
- **General-Purpose Design**: Ability to operate in human-designed environments
- **Research Platform**: Understanding human cognition through human-like embodiment
